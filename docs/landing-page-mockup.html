Frequently asked questions

Isn’t this the same as the missed-call text-back in GoHighLevel or Podium?

No — those tools send a text and hope the caller replies. Callverted answers the call live, like a receptionist would, asks restoration-specific questions, scores the lead, and emails you a job-value estimate — all before you’d have finished listening to a voicemail.

Does it replace my team, or my phone number?

Neither. You keep your number, and your team always gets the first chance to answer. Callverted only steps in when a call goes unanswered.

What happens on a call it can’t handle?

If a caller asks for something outside the intake flow, or asks for a person, Callverted says so plainly and can transfer them live if you’ve set up a transfer number.

What verticals does Callverted support right now?

Water, fire, and mold restoration is fully live. HVAC and personal injury law are in development.

How does the scoring work?

Restoration-specific rules calculate the score (e.g. Category 3 water + hardwood floors = high urgency). GPT-4o writes the plain-English reasoning — it never changes the numbers.

What’s the setup time?

About 30 minutes to configure your account. We provision your dedicated number within one business day.

Ready to stop losing leads to voicemail?

Set up in about 30 minutes. First lead recovery is on us.
