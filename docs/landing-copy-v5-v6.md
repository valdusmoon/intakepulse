## Positioning (locked)

**Category:** Lead recovery for home-service trades. NOT a receptionist, answering
service, chatbot, scheduler, CRM, or knowledge-base bot.

**Core promise:** Capture every inbound opportunity → document it → qualify it →
prioritize it → tell the team exactly who to call back first.

**The repeated mental model (say it across the page):**
> **Many ways in. One ranked list out.**

**Channels in:** missed calls · answered calls · website inquiries.
**Outputs:** **Job** (qualified + scored Hot/Warm/Cool + callback packet) · **Message**
(cleanly documented, routed, never scored) · **Screened** (obvious junk, logged, never a lead).

**Rules:** messages are never scored. Never screen if there is any plausible service need.
The AI captures, it does not improvise — callers only hear pricing you approved, word for word.

**Style:** plain, direct, short sentences, outcome-led, minimal em dashes. No jargon on the page.

## 2. The Leak
- **Eyebrow:** THE LEAK
- **Headline:** There's more than one way to lose a job.
- **Support:** Every inbound opportunity that goes uncaptured, undocumented, or unprioritized is a job your competitor can win.
- **Three cards** — three distinct failure modes (not captured → not documented → not prioritized), each a photo + one line + a real call-log chip:
  1. **The call rings out.** Nobody's free, or it's 11 PM. The caller doesn't leave a voicemail — they dial the next company on the list. *(chip: Missed · on a job)*
  2. **The details never get written down.** The call your team answered, or the web form nobody worked, lives in someone's memory until it's gone. *(chip: Web lead · never worked)*
  3. **The big job waits behind the small one.** Five callbacks in the queue and no way to tell the $9k emergency from the $150 tune-up, so you call back in the wrong order. *(chip: Hot lead · called back 4th)*

## 3. How It Works
- **Eyebrow:** HOW IT WORKS
- **Headline:** Every way in. One ranked list out.
- **Subhead:** One system on your phone line and your website makes sure nothing that comes in gets lost.
- **Four steps:**
  1. **Your team gets the call first.** You publish one Callverted number. Every call rings your existing phones first, so nothing changes for the work you already catch.
  2. **Callverted captures the rest.** Missed, after-hours, and overflow calls, the calls your team answers, and every website inquiry.
  3. **It sorts and scores.** Each one becomes a qualified job, a tidy message, or a screened non-lead. Real jobs get scored by urgency and value.
  4. **Your team gets a ranked list.** Callback-ready lead packets, highest-priority job at the top, so you call the money back first.

## 10. FAQ  *(full category-clarifying set)*
1. **Is this an AI receptionist?** No. An answering service takes a message. Callverted captures the opportunity, sorts a real job from a message, scores and ranks the jobs, and tells your team who to call back first. It captures calls and website inquiries, not just missed calls.
2. **Does it replace my phone number?** No. You publish a Callverted number that rings your team first on every call. It only steps in when nobody picks up.
3. **Does it handle website leads too?** Yes. Add the widget or share your intake link. Those go through the same qualification, scoring, and sorting as phone calls.
4. **What about the calls my team answers?** Switch on capture and the calls your team takes get transcribed, summarized, and scored too, so the details stop living in someone's head. The audio is deleted once transcribed, never stored.
5. **Will the AI answer questions or book appointments?** No, and that's on purpose. It captures, it does not improvise. Callers only hear pricing you approved, word for word. If there's no approved answer, it says your team will follow up.
6. **What happens on an emergency?** It separates a true emergency from a routine ask, flags it, and alerts your on-call instead of parking it until morning.
7. **How fast can we launch?** About 30 minutes of setup: call flow, service area, pricing rules, and test calls. We walk through it with you.
